About Me

My name is Julie Saltrelli and I came to web development and design through a long winding journey starting with a liberal arts degree, teaching English abroad in Italy and Argentina and then working in sales for a tech company in Portland, OR.

My daughter was my inspiration for learning to code. Watching her perseverance while learning to walk and talk made me realize that I can learn coding languages and how to design websites. Being reborn as a mom gave me the courage to rebuild my career from the ground up.

I value clean and concise code,
thoughtful layouts
and a pleasing client experience.
